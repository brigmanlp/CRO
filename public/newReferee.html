NC Soccer Referee Association Registration



This registration is for referees interested in working US Soccer and US Club sanctioned matches this includes: NCYSA Classic Level Soccer, US Development Academy, NCYSA Select Level Soccer. Important Information to read BEFORE registering for the NC Referee Program:

  • You MUST include the following numbers WITH the parenthesis in the “Assignor Number” section of the registration (162) () ()
  • You MUST email Kevin Broadley, Mark Schumacher and Jeremy Mikel once registration is complete to inform them you are ready to be assigned soccer matches.
  • You MUST enable sharing if you are a part of multiple Arbiter Groups.
  • Once in your Arbiter Account, Click on the Profile Tab - Then the Sharing Link - Check ALL the Boxes available - Click Save Your Arbiter Profile MUST have a head shot style photo, phone number, email address, mailing address and SSN in order to officiate.
